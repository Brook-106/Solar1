Mars Facts
  • Mars is known as the Red Planet because of iron oxide on its surface.
  • Mars has two moons named Phobos and Deimos.
  • Olympus Mons is the tallest volcano in the Solar System.
  • A day on Mars lasts about 24.6 Earth hours.
  • Scientists believe Mars had liquid water in the ancient past.
  • Mars is explored by orbiters, landers, and robotic rovers.
